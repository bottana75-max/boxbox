AI Predictions

When you request an AI race prediction, BoxBox sends the following data to OpenAI (api.openai.com):

  • Current F1 championship standings (public data)
  • Recent race results (public data)
  • Circuit information (public data)

No personal information about you is sent to OpenAI. OpenAI's privacy policy applies.

Third-party APIs

BoxBox retrieves publicly available F1 data from OpenF1 (openf1.org) and Jolpica F1 API. Your device makes requests to their servers; no personal data is included in these requests.
